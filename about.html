About SafeEats

Committed to ensuring food safety and quality for schools and communities.

Who We Are

SafeEats is a dedicated organization committed to ensuring the safety and quality of food served in schools and communities. Our mission is to protect the health and well-being of children by providing innovative food monitoring and compliance solutions.

Our Mission

To create a safer food system by empowering vendors, schools, and communities with tools to monitor, verify, and improve food safety standards.

Our Core Values

Health & Safety

We prioritize the health and safety of children and communities.

Sustainability

We promote sustainable practices for a better future.

Collaboration

We work together with vendors, schools, and communities.

Innovation

We embrace innovation to improve food safety systems.
